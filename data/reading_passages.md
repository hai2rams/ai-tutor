## Fractions — Sharing Pizza

Maya cut a pizza into 8 equal slices. She ate 2 slices and gave 3 slices to her brother.
That means Maya ate 2/8 of the pizza and her brother ate 3/8 of the pizza.
Together they ate 5/8 of the pizza because the denominators are the same.

## Fractions — Equivalent Fractions

One half of a rectangle is the same as two quarters.
If you split each half into two equal parts, you get four parts in total and two are shaded.
So 1/2 = 2/4.
